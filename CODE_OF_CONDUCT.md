# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

The purpose of this Code of Conduct is to maintain a respectful, inclusive, and welcoming environment for all contributors and users of PawMatch.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants should:

* Communicate respectfully
* Accept and provide constructive feedback
* Collaborate in good faith
* Show professionalism and empathy
* Follow project standards and guidelines

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following actions are prohibited:

* Harassment or discrimination
* Personal, political, or offensive attacks
* Threatening or abusive language or behavior
* Sexual or inappropriate content
* Publishing private information without consent
* Spamming, trolling, or disruption

---

## Reporting a Problem

If you witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Record details of the incident when possible

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may:

* Issue warnings
* Remove content
* Temporarily or permanently remove contributors from the project
* Reject contributions when necessary

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all:

* Project spaces such as repository, issues, discussions, and pull requests
* Project-related communication channels
